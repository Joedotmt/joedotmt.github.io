Here are some of the things I've worked on

HopDodge is a 3D puzzle platforming game. Create and share levels using the steam workshop. You have all the freedom you need to create your own adventures.

SASSLS is a free, open-source Library Management System designed to help Maltese schools manage their libraries efficiently, in an effort to promote reading among secondary schools in Malta. Available on GitHub

The Malta Veterans Association commissioned me to develop a website and membership management platform to streamline operations and serve as the official information hub for veterans of the Malta Armed Forces.

Relaxing Mini Golf is a work-in-progress 2D puzzle game that combines the fun of mini golf with engaging puzzle mechanics. Well- if you can even call it golf.
